# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want BPMax-Air to be an open, respectful, and technically rigorous project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- giving actionable technical feedback
- assuming good intent
- discussing tradeoffs directly and respectfully
- keeping issues and pull requests focused on the work

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- bad-faith argumentation
- doxxing or publication of private information
- spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
